OUR STORY

Built on insight. Driven by care.

01

It Started with
a Simple Idea

During the pandemic, it became clear that traditional hotel stays often felt impersonal and limiting. People needed space, privacy, and the comforts of home—whether for work or for rest. That insight shaped the creation of Holt Accommodation Solutions.

02

Our Approach

We go beyond sourcing accommodation. We manage everything, end to end.

From maintenance and housekeeping to guest support and reporting, our team handles it all—so you can focus on what matters most.

One clear monthly payment. No upfront commitment until accommodation is secured.

03

Our Vision
& Mission

Vision: To make quality accommodation effortless, wherever work or life takes people.

Mission: To make every stay effortless, supporting guests from arrival to departure.

Curated stays for teams and travellers. Workstays, relocations and luxury retreats, managed with care.
